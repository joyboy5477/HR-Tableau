HR Analytics Dashboard Project

Welcome to our HR analytics dashboard project. Through this project, we aim to provide HR professionals with the tools and insights needed to make data-driven decisions regarding employee retention, development, and recruitment.

Problem Statement

Our goal is to analyze employee data to identify trends and patterns that can help improve HR strategies. By analyzing attrition rates, gender distribution, department-wise attrition, age groups, job satisfaction, and education field-wise attrition, we aim to provide actionable insights for optimizing HR operations.

Dashboard Content

Our dashboard includes a range of visualizations and metrics to provide a comprehensive view of our HR data:

  • KPI Overview: Employee count, attrition count, attrition rate, active employees, and average age.
  • Attrition by Gender: Lollipop chart showing attrition rates by gender.
  • Department-wise Attrition: Pie chart illustrating attrition distribution across departments.
  • Employee Age Groups: Bar graph depicting distribution of employees by age groups.
  • Job Satisfaction: Square chart representing job satisfaction ratings.
  • Education Field-wise Attrition (Bar Graph): Vertical bars showing attrition rates by education field.
  • Education Field-wise Attrition (Donut Plot): Donut plot visualizing attrition percentages by education field.

Conclusion

Our HR analytics dashboard empowers HR professionals with actionable insights to enhance employee engagement, retention, and overall organizational performance. By leveraging Tableau's visualization capabilities, we can make data-driven decisions that positively impact our workforce.
